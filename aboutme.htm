         amiga module music :

- module songs (songs in .mod format) are songs that are made on tracker softwares on the amiga computer, there are multiple tracker softwares on the amiga like ultimate soundtracker, noisetracker and most popular one being protracker

- it works by playing sequences of samples in real-time and has only 4 audio channels to work with and multiple samples cannot be played in one audio channel, 1st and 4th channel will be panned to the left while the rest panned to the right, despite the audio chip limitation (amiga paula), it's actually very good for its time (1985) since it's actually able to play pcm samples which is a rarity back then and many great songs are created with it, even some people still use protracker nowadays (like me!)

-> archival website full of module music as well as tracker songs made on pc : mod archive
