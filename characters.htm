        xeno (xen)       

   

        xeno (shorter is xen) is a full-fluff protogen with purple display who has electric ability as well as really like to zap other furs playfully and glow up their bones and likes to be zapped himself, his electricity doesn't hurt a lot but can generate a lot of muscles spasms and twitches, he can also adjust his electric power to be more powerful and can cause pain in cases of self-defense or to furs who are way too into it, he also can shoots a zap into the sky to summon a lightning that shocks more powerfully than his zap power itself, his zap power can also turn the zapped into a clean skeleton if powerful enough, he usually cuddles with it before revive zapping them back to normal

        for his personality, he's friendly, loyal and care for his friends but at the same time can be silly and mischievous at times especially during his playful zap sessions, overall nice to be with if you don't mind being hugged from behind and get zapped multiple times

        despite having electric ability, he's not immune to electricity himself as he only has a electric shield at his chest, he still can be zapped normally and even passing out after too many zaps, unlike others he's still able to live as skeleton since his skeleton is his actual form and does not take away his electric power whatsoever

        also he recharges through sleeping, zapping too much can make him feel tired after a while
