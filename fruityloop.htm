about the music player, it's a flash "movie" i made by myself using macromedia/adobe flash 8 from 2005

for the green-ish yellow themed players, they are songs that sound fun, happier and overall less aggressive

for the redder ones, they are aggressive-sounding songs and usually fit in more action-like scenarios

 

(don't have flash ? get it here if you are on chrome)

 

## buttons instruction :

- play / pause button (the big middle one) : pretty much pause or un-pause the song

- stop button (top left) : stop the song and winds the cursor back to 0:00

- loop button (top right) : enabling looping, 1st click enables 1 loop only, 2nd click will enable 2 loops, 3rd one will enable infinite loops

after the 3rd click, it will turn off the looping

- back button (bottom left) : go back by 8 seconds, song continues like usual if playing, song won't play when paused

- skip button (bottom right) : skip ahead by 8 seconds, song continues like usual if playing, song won't play when paused

- volume button (on the very right) : select the volume by 100%, 75%, 50%, 25% and 0% (muted)

- volume slider (on top of volume button) : select volume from 0% to 100% dynamically

- playback cursor (on playback bar) : select specifically which part to play, note it will pause the song when doing so and start again once stopped selecting

 

also quick note : speed of text, visualizers and overall the whole player is depended on the bpm (beats-per-minute) of the chosen song
